Description

Balak is an app, specially created by the student of shivalik college of engineering for the students of shivalik college of engineering. Balak shows all the recent and the upcoming events organized in the shivalik college of engineering. It also shows the recent activities of the different clubs of the college.

Students can see the details of the different events and can also enroll in any events directly through this app. Also the admin or the clubs can send the real-time notifications directly to all the enrolled students in this app related to any events or activities.
